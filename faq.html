FAQ

Most asked questions:

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? step 1: go to https://mynetpay.nl/ or scan the QR-code in the printer room.
step 2: click on 'mobility print'.
step 3: choose your operatating system and follow the instructions.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ? step 1: swipe your studentcard
step 2: open te printer and place your paper against the Arrow in the corner
step 3 close the printer and chooce a subject for your scan
step 4: press start and log-out.
How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop? step 1: go to the hz webshop page
step 2: click on the products you would like to order.
step 3: make sure you have the right amount of product you like to order.
step 4: locate your shopping cart
step 5: order your products
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? step 1: park your vehicle at the parking lot "kousteensedijk"
step 2: walk to the hz building and follow your lessons
step 3: ask for an exit ticket at the joint research centre counter when you are want to go home.
step 4: walk to your car and drive away
How can you book a project space? step 1: go to HZ.Portal
step 2: Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal
step 3: Click on Reserve
step 4: Choose the correct category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve. If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview.
step 5: Select the day when the resource is borrowed
step 6: Indicate, by dragging with the left mouse button, the time period when the resource is borrowed
step 7: Click on Next
step 8: Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click on Next
step 9: Fill in the necessary information and click on Confirm reservation
